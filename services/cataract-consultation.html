Expert Cataract Consultation

Safe and effective solutions for clear, brighter vision.

Specialized Care

Cataracts are the clouding of the eye's natural lens, which can lead to blurred vision and increased sensitivity to glare. At Sudrishti Eye Clinic, we offer comprehensive cataract screenings. Dr. Preeti provides professional guidance on whether surgery is necessary and what kind of intraocular lens (IOL) would best suit your needs.

The Problem: Why don't ignore cataracts?

Untreated cataracts can significantly impair your quality of life, making daily tasks like driving or reading impossible. As cataracts progress, the eye's internal pressure could increase, leading to secondary glaze or even permanent vision loss.


The Solution: Modern Treatment Options

Advanced surgical techniques allow for quick, painless cataract removal with minimal downtime. We use modern diagnostic tools to ensure the best surgical plan and offer premium intraocular lens options for superior outcomes.
