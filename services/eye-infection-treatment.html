Eye Infection & Allergy Treatment

Relief from discomfort, redness, and irritation.

Specialized Treatment

Eye infections like conjunctivitis (pink eye), keratitis, and blepharitis require prompt medical attention to avoid permanent damage. Common symptoms include persistent redness, itching, discharge, and blurred vision. At Sudrishti Eye Clinic, we offer rapid diagnosis and effective treatment plans tailored to the root cause of your infection.

The Problem: Don't ignore irritations!

Often, people mistake serious infections for minor allergies and use over-the-counter drops without expert advice. This can aggravate the condition and lead to scarring or vision loss.


The Solution: Accurate Diagnosis

Dr. Preeti evaluates each case using slit-lamp examination to precisely identify the infectious agent—whether it's bacterial, viral, or fungal. We provide specialized care to restore your eye health quickly and safely.
