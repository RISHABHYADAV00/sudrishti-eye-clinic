Comprehensive Eye Checkup

Professional diagnostics for clear and healthy vision.

Detailed Explanation

At Sudrishti Eye Clinic, a comprehensive eye checkup is more than just reading letters on a chart. Our expert team uses advanced diagnostic equipment to evaluate the health of your eyes and the quality of your vision. This includes checking for glaucoma, macular degeneration, and other potentially serious conditions that may not have obvious symptoms in the early stages.

The Problem: Why is it necessary?

Many eye diseases, such as glaucoma or diabetic retinopathy, develop silently without any pain or noticeable vision change until it's too late. Regular checkups are crucial even if you think your vision is perfect.


The Solution: Our Approach

Our thorough examination includes visual acuity tests, retinal scans, and intraocular pressure measurements. We provide a complete report of your eye health and recommend the best course of action, whether it's lifestyle changes, corrective lenses, or specialized medical treatment.
